Theorem

Every positive iterate of a constant function is a constant function. Proof does not actually require primsubs. FORALL z(I) FORALL g(II).ITERATE+ [LAMBDA x(I) z] g IMPLIES FORALL x.g x = z

∀ z ι , g ι → ι . ITERATE+ ( λ x ι . z , g ) ⇒ ∀ x ι . g ( x ) = z
