Conceptinteger-eq-conditional
The conditional on integers. It takes four arguments: The first two are integers, the last two are arbitrary terms. If the first two are equal, then the value of the complex expression is that the third, else the fourth argument.

Conceptinteger-less-conditional
The inequality conditional on integers. It takes four arguments: The first two are integers, the last two are arbitrary terms. If the first is less than the second one, then the value of the complex expression is that the third, else the fourth argument.
