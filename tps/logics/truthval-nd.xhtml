1. Natural Deduction Rules for Truth Values.

imports from: truthval, inference-rules
Conceptfalse-elim( Definition)
Definition

The inference rule known as 'ex falso quotlibet' or 'falsity-elimination': A . It allows to derive an arbitrary formula from F .

Concepttrue-intro
Definition

This is the inference rule for 'truth-introduction' ( T ) . It allows to introduce T anywhere.
