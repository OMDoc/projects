1. Substitution

Conceptsubstitute
The elementary substitution constructor, it takes two arguments: the replacement term, the variable.
Conceptapply
The applicatio operator for substitutions, it takes two arguments: a substitution and a term.
Example

The expression ( ) is the substitution operator that substitutes the term B for the variable X . It can be applied to a term by the substitution application operator. ( ) this denotes the term that is obtained by replacing all free occurrences of the variable X with B in A .

Conceptsubstlist
The constructor for complex substitutions: it takes n arguments that are elementary substitutions.
