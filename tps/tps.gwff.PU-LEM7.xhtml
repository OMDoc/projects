Lemma 7 from Dana Scott's Domain Theory Notes on Pairing Unpairing Algebras. PAIR-UNP-ALG(O(AA)(AA)(AAA)A) Z(A) P(AAA) L(AA) R(AA) IMPLIES FORALL s(A).PU-S(OA(AA)(AA)(AAA)A) Z P L R s IMPLIES FORALL b(A).PU-B(OA(AA)(AA)(AAA)A) Z P L R b IMPLIES FORALL u(A).PU-SUBTREES(OAA(AA)(AA)(AAA)A) Z P L R t(A) [P b u] IMPLIES PU-S Z P L R u
