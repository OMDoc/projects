Theorem

A version of the first homomorphism theorem just in terms of sets and equivalence relations. See Benji Shults' message of 12 Feb 1998 Wos' 8th research problem: 'an inference rule for set thery?' In the statement of this problem, he gives a challenge problem: G/Ker(f) is isomorphic to H where f : G -> H is an onto homomorphism Note: could just formulate this in terms of an equivalence relation and its quotient set.
