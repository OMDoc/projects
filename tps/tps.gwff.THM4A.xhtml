Theorem

FORALL P(OI) FORALL Q(OI) [FORALL X(I) [P X EQUIV Q X] IMPLIES P = Q] IMPLIES FORALL T(O(OI)) FORALL U(OI) FORALL V(OI).T [SETUNION.LAMBDA W(OI).W = U OR W = V] IMPLIES T.U UNION V

∀ P ι → o , Q ι → o . ∀ X ι . P ( X ) ≡ Q ( X ) ⇒ ( P = Q ) ⇒ ∀ T ι → o → o , U ι → o , V ι → o . T ( SETUNION ( λ W ι → o . W = U ∨ W = V ) ) ⇒ T ( U UNION V )
