Theorem

FORALL y(I) [FORALL x(I) P(OII) x y IMPLIES FORALL u(I) Q(OII) u y] IMPLIES EXISTS z(I).EXISTS v(I) P v z IMPLIES EXISTS w(I).P z w OR Q w z
