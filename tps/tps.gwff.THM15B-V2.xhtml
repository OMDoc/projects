imports from: tps.abbr.ITERATE.plus, tps.abbr.UNIQUE-FIXPOINT, tps.abbr.FIXPOINT
Theorem

FORALL f(II).EXISTS g(II) [ITERATE+ f g AND EXISTS x(I) UNIQUE-FIXPOINT(OI(II)) g x] IMPLIES EXISTS y(I) FIXPOINT(OI(II)) f y

∀ f ι → ι . ∃ g ι → ι . ITERATE+ ( f , g ) ∧ ∃ x ι . UNIQUE-FIXPOINT ( g , x ) ⇒ ∃ y ι . FIXPOINT ( f , y )
