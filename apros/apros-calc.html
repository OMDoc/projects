Deduction Rules for Apros Theorem Prover
Michael Kohlhase
20020227
This theory provides symbols for the deduction rules of the Apros theorem prover developed at the Philosophy Department of Carnegie Mellon University.
ConceptAllE(Definition)

The universal elimination rule (AllE).

Definition

The universal elimination rule (AllE). This has the following form:

(∀x.P(x))
[a/x]P(x)
ConceptAllI(Definition)

The universal introduction rule (AllI).

Definition

The universal introduction rule (AllI). This has the following form:

P(x)
(∀x.P(x))

where a does not occur in the formula (∀x.P(x)) or in any assumption of the scope.
